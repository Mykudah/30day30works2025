Video 1: Live performance
Rock, Paper, Scissors
More Info

Title: Rock, Paper, Scissors
Year made: 2022
Duration: 5 minutes
Material: Violin, charcoal, and algorithms that respond to body motion to create a digital image

A three-way collaboration: A violinist begins playing music. A charcoal artist responds to the music by drawing. An algorithm tracks her body movements, creating a digital image of snow dispersing on a screen, to which the violinist responds by altering his music, completing a dynamic loop of interaction.

Video 2: Concept video for proposed performance
Pathways of Light
More Info

Title: Pathways of Light (Proposed Work for Whitechapel Gallery)
Year: 2025 (currently under development)
Duration: Variable (suggested 15 minutes)
Material: Hats made from broken Perspex mirrors and eggshells (similar to the 2022 installation), light, and sound

This proposed performance builds on the 2022 installation of the same title, incorporating wearable sculptures and dynamic light and sound to create an immersive experience.
