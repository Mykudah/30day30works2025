Day 3
Brief: Reuse, Recycle, Reclaim

Response: Humpty Dumpty version 2.0. A cracked egg has been "put back" again, but not in the form you'd expect. She is version 2.0.


More Info

Date made: 3rd February 2025
Material: Broken and waste eggshells

12.9 Billion
The number of eggs consumed in the UK each year. The total eggshells that go into landfill is ninety-three thousand metric tonnes. That is the weight of around thirty thousand elephants.

An eggshell is ninety-five percent calcium carbonate. My sculpture is made from eggshell collected from restaurants, washed, powdered, and treated. They use natural binders and materials that make them carbon-negative.

With the myriad of technological changes in our world, like the cracking of an eggshell, new forces are emerging. The choice is ours: What direction do we want to take for the future of the world?
